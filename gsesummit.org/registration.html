Registration

STUDENTS: INR 5000/- (USD 75)

CHAPERONE: INR 2000/- (USD 30)

GSES - 2019 Form

The summit will be organised from Friday, February 22nd 2019 to Sunday, February 24th 2019. Once the students register, they will receive an information packet that they need to go through before coming to the Summit. The deadline for registration is February 15th 2019. Students can register for the summit by signing up at the form provided above titled, 'GSES - 2019 Registration Form'. The registration for adults (chaperones) attending GSES can be completed at the same link in the 'Chaperone Registration' section.
