Attendees

Participants will work in cross-school teams that will have the opportunity to ideate, research, pitch and refine their ideas and produce clear proposals and multimedia material that can be used for further sharing, promotion and development.

Participating schools are encouraged to bring:

  • Existing service learning or social entrepreneurial projects and ideas from their own campus for re-working and further development
  • Social challenges within their community
    • Existing ways these challenges are being addressed at school
    • Innovative solutions that can address this challenge more effectively

We invite students and teachers across the world to consider attending this unique event. The summit will be an opportunity to work with and learn from others and broaden personal and school vision from service learning to social entrepreneurship.
